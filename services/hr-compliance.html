Compliance Coverage

India's labour law landscape is complex — with central statutes, state-level variations, and industry-specific regulations all in play. Our compliance practice covers the full spectrum, ensuring you're protected at every level.

What We Cover

  • ESI (Employees' State Insurance) — registration, contribution management, filings
  • PF (Provident Fund) — enrolment, monthly returns, UAN management
  • POSH (Prevention of Sexual Harassment) — policy, ICC formation, training, annual reports
  • CLRA (Contract Labour Regulation & Abolition) — registration, licence management
  • Shops & Establishments Act — state-wise compliance and renewals
  • Minimum Wages Act — state-specific applicability and revision tracking
  • Maternity Benefit Act — policy alignment and leave management
  • HR policy manuals — employee handbooks, disciplinary procedures, grievance mechanisms
  • Employment contracts — offer letters, appointment letters, NDAs, service agreements
  • HRIS implementation — selecting and deploying the right system for your size

Why It Matters

A single labour tribunal claim can cost ₹5–50 lakhs and months of management time. A POSH non-compliance notice carries criminal liability. Our compliance practice is an insurance policy — one that also makes your HR operations faster, cleaner, and more scalable.

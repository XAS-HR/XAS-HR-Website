Our Philosophy

Performance management works when it's continuous, transparent, and tied directly to what the organisation is trying to achieve. We design frameworks that create clarity about expectations, provide honest feedback, and make reward decisions feel fair — not political.

What We Build

  • KRA and KPI framework design — aligned to your business strategy and cascaded to every role
  • OKR (Objectives & Key Results) implementation — for organisations wanting a more agile approach
  • Appraisal cycle design — timelines, rating scales, calibration process, and documentation
  • 360° feedback system — peer, upward, and downward feedback with structured templates
  • Performance Improvement Plans (PIPs) — fair, documented, and legally defensible
  • Reward and recognition architecture — variable pay, incentives, spot bonuses, non-monetary recognition
  • Compensation benchmarking — market data and salary band design
  • Increment and promotion frameworks — criteria, governance, and communication guides

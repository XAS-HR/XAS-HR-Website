Strategic HR Partnership

Whether you're scaling rapidly, going through a merger, restructuring, or simply realising that your current HR approach isn't fit for the next stage of growth — our advisory team becomes your strategic partner. We bring the frameworks, the experience, and the independence to see your people challenges clearly and act on them decisively.

What We Advise On

  • HR function design — building or restructuring your internal HR team
  • Organisational design — structure, spans of control, reporting lines, and role clarity
  • Workforce planning — headcount modelling, skills forecasting, and capacity planning
  • HR transformation — moving from reactive administration to strategic people management
  • Fractional CHRO — retained senior HR leadership for organisations not yet ready for a full-time CHRO
  • Merger & acquisition HR — due diligence, integration planning, and culture harmonisation
  • Change management — structured programmes for major organisational transitions
  • HR technology strategy — HRIS selection, integration, and roadmap
  • People analytics — building the metrics and dashboards that drive better people decisions
  • Board-level HR reporting — designing the people metrics that belong in the boardroom

The Fractional CHRO Model

For many growing organisations, a full-time CHRO is premature — but the strategic HR capability is urgently needed. Our Fractional CHRO service gives you senior HR leadership on a retained basis: attending leadership meetings, driving the people agenda, and building the HR function from the inside — without the full-time salary cost.
